# Community Funding Spec

Donation model, optional supporter perks, crowdfunded feature roadmap.

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Core app is free
The core Nah experience SHALL be free for all users. There are no paywalls for essential features.

#### Scenario: New user signup
- **WHEN** a new user joins Nah
- **THEN** they have full access to all core features without payment

#### Scenario: No feature gating
- **WHEN** user uses the app
- **THEN** no essential features are locked behind payment

### Requirement: Voluntary donation support
Users SHALL be able to voluntarily support Nah through donations via Patreon, Open Collective, GitHub Sponsors, or in-app.

#### Scenario: Finding donation options
- **WHEN** user wants to support Nah
- **THEN** they can easily find donation links/options

#### Scenario: No donation pressure
- **WHEN** user chooses not to donate
- **THEN** their experience is not degraded or nagged

### Requirement: Optional supporter perks
Supporters MAY receive optional perks that don't affect core functionality — badges, exclusive themes, early access to new features.

#### Scenario: Supporter badge
- **WHEN** user becomes a supporter
- **THEN** they may display an optional supporter badge on their profile

#### Scenario: Exclusive themes
- **WHEN** supporter perks include themes
- **THEN** supporters can access additional visual themes

#### Scenario: Perks don't create inequality
- **WHEN** perks are offered
- **THEN** they are cosmetic/optional and don't create feature disparity

### Requirement: Transparent financial reporting
Nah SHALL publish transparent financial reports showing income and expenses.

#### Scenario: Viewing finances
- **WHEN** anyone wants to see Nah's finances
- **THEN** public reports show donations received and how funds are used

#### Scenario: Regular reporting
- **WHEN** financial reports are published
- **THEN** they are updated at least quarterly

### Requirement: Community-driven roadmap
The feature roadmap SHALL be influenced by community input. Supporters may have additional input mechanisms.

#### Scenario: Public roadmap
- **WHEN** users want to see planned features
- **THEN** a public roadmap is available

#### Scenario: Feature requests
- **WHEN** users want to suggest features
- **THEN** there's a mechanism to submit and vote on suggestions

### Requirement: Crowdfunded feature development
Specific features MAY be crowdfunded — the community collectively funds development of desired features.

#### Scenario: Feature crowdfunding
- **WHEN** a feature is proposed for crowdfunding
- **THEN** users can pledge towards its development

#### Scenario: Funding goal met
- **WHEN** a feature's funding goal is met
- **THEN** development of that feature is prioritized

### Requirement: No venture capital dependency
Nah SHALL NOT take venture capital or investment that requires growth-at-all-costs or exit-focused decisions.

#### Scenario: Funding decisions
- **WHEN** funding opportunities arise
- **THEN** community sustainability is prioritized over rapid growth

#### Scenario: Ownership structure
- **WHEN** Nah's ownership is examined
- **THEN** it remains community-oriented (non-profit, cooperative, or similar structure)
